# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in this project.

## Current Contribution Policy

At this stage, this repository is maintainer-only.

- External pull requests are currently not accepted.
- Feature requests and bug reports may be discussed, but implementation decisions remain with maintainers.
- Maintainers may close PRs/issues as `not planned` when they do not fit the roadmap.

## Why

The project is in an early stabilization phase before wider community collaboration.

## Future Updates

This policy may change in future releases. Any changes will be announced in this file.
